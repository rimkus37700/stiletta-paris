Dispatch

Shipping in France

  • Free shipping on all orders to metropolitan France
  • Dispatched within 24 to 48 business hours of payment confirmation
  • Estimated delivery: 3 to 5 business days
  • Tracking number provided by e-mail upon dispatch
  • Collection point delivery available on request
International

International shipping

  • Shipping to the European Union and worldwide
  • Shipping costs calculated by destination
  • Estimated: 5 to 10 business days depending on country
  • Package insurance included
  • Any applicable customs duties are the recipient's responsibility
Packaging

The Stiletta Paris gift box

  • Each creation is presented in a rigid black box branded Stiletta Paris
  • Velvet pouch, tissue paper, ivory satin ribbon
  • Certificate of authenticity signed by the House
  • Leather care and maintenance booklet
  • Optional handwritten personalised message on request
Returns

Return procedure

  • Returns accepted within 14 calendar days of receipt
  • The product must be in its original condition, unworn, in complete original packaging
  • Notify your request by e-mail to contact@stiletta-paris.com
  • A return label will be sent to you within 48 hours
  • Return shipping costs are the buyer's responsibility (except for defective products)
  • Full refund within 14 days of receiving the returned parcel

For any questions regarding your order or a return, our team is available at contact@stiletta-paris.com.

Response time: 48 business hours.
