The following payment methods are accepted:

In accordance with article L221-18 of the French Consumer Code, the Buyer has a period of 14 calendar days from receipt of the goods to exercise their right of withdrawal, without providing reasons.

To exercise this right, the Buyer must notify their decision by e-mail to contact@stiletta-paris.com before the deadline expires. The product must be returned in its original condition, in its original packaging, with all accessories.
