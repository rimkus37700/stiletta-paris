The Creation

The Stiletta Palm is the first creation of the House STILETTA PARIS.

A piece conceived as a signature, where every detail expresses an intention — the architecture of the stiletto heel transcribed into the structure of a bag.

We believe one strong piece is worth more than a scattered collection.

The Stiletta Palm embodies this vision: a clear, assumed, timeless identity.

Craftsmanship & Quality

Each piece is crafted in premium leather carefully selected for its structure and durability.

Our creations are developed with specialised partners, selected for their exacting standards and expertise.

Yes. The Stiletta Palm is designed to last over time, both in its structure and in the quality of its materials.

Order & Shipping

Orders are dispatched within 2 to 5 business days. A tracking number is provided as soon as your order ships.

Yes, STILETTA PARIS offers international shipping. Delivery times and costs vary by destination.

Returns & Service

You have 14 days from receipt to return the product, provided it is unworn and in its original condition.

Our customer service team is here to help.

Contact: contact@stiletta-paris.com

Care

We recommend avoiding moisture and cleaning with a soft cloth.

The bag should be stored in its dust bag after use. A care booklet is provided with each piece.

Exclusivity

Yes. The Stiletta Palm is offered as a first edition, available in a limited quantity of 250 pieces.

New creations will emerge, in keeping with the House's DNA: singularity, balance and signature.
